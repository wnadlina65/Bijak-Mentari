Bijak Mentari Privacy Policy

What do you do with my information?

CONSENT

When you provide us with personal information to complete a transaction, verify your credit card, place an order, arrange for a delivery or return a purchase, we imply that you consent to our collecting it and using it for that specific reason only. After you opt-in, if you change your mind, you may withdraw your consent for us to contact you, for the continued collection, use or disclosure of your information, at any time, by contacting us at bijakmentari53@gmail.com or mailing us at: Bijak Mentari B-2-163, GM KLANG WHOLESALE CITY, JALAN KASUARINA 1, BANDAR BOTANIK Klang Selangor Malaysia 41200.



DISCLOSURE

Do you disclose my information?

We may disclose your personal information if we are required by law to do so or if you violate our Terms of Service.



THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Do you disclose my information?

In general, the third-party providers we use will only collect, use, and disclose your information to the extent necessary to allow them to perform the services they provide to us.
